# As Featured In — G&D Kézműves Cukrászda és Pékség / Szó ami Szó

*Compiled April 2026 — covers all known awards, press features, media appearances, and notable recognitions for the G&D / Szó ami Szó brand and its founders Szó Gellért (pastry chef) and Szó Dániel (baker).*

---

## 🏆 Awards & Competition Results

### Szó Gellért — Pastry Competitions

| Year | Award | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| **2015** | 🥇 **Magyarország Tortája** (Hungary's Cake of the Year) | Won with *"Pannonhalmi sárgabarack-pálinkás karamelltorta"* (Pannonhalma apricot brandy caramel cake). Notably, he submitted two recipes and won 1st and 2nd place simultaneously. |
| **2016** | 🥇 **Magyarország Tortája** (2nd consecutive win) | Won with *"Az Őrség Zöld Aranya"* (The Green Gold of Őrség). The only back-to-back winner in the competition's modern era. |
| **Post-2016** | 🧑‍⚖️ **Jury Member, Magyarország Tortája** | After his two victories, Szó Gellért was invited to serve as a jury member for the national cake competition, alongside Michelin-starred chef Sárközi Ákos (Borkonyha). |

> Source: nool.hu, turizmus.com, welovebalaton.hu, karpatiaettermek.hu

---

### Szó Dániel — Baking Competitions

| Year | Award | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| **2017** | 🥇 **Az Ország Legjobb Croissantja** (Best Croissant in Hungary) | Won at the **Kenyérlelke Fesztivál** (Soul of Bread Festival). The sourdough starter was cultivated by Dániel, and Gellért did the lamination — a true brotherly collaboration. |

> Source: welovebalaton.hu, hirbalaton.hu (Street Kitchen), olyanjo.hu, kekesonline.hu

## 🌍 International Recognition

| Recognition | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Erasmus+ "Face of the Year"** | Szó Gellért was selected as the **Face of the Year** of the Erasmus+ program, recognised at the European level in **Brussels**. He was named the **Hungarian Ambassador of the Erasmus+ programme**, representing the programme to inspire younger generations. ## 📋 Additional Notable Mentions

- **Gellért trained in Italy and France** before his competition wins, and later attended a masterclass in Poland with a Spanish world champion pastry chef — coverage in Kékes Online, 2017.
- **REÖK Kézműves Cukrászda (Szeged)**: Szó Gellért served as head pastry chef, and the venue — under his creative direction — won *Év Kávézója 2019, Év Baristája 2019, Év Kávéja 2019* in the Kávékorzó Magazin national public vote, beating major Budapest venues. French Ambassador **Pascale Andréani** held an event there.
- **Own mill**: The G&D operation mills its own flour for bread production, a detail noted by multiple press outlets as exceptional.
- The Balatonkenese location outgrew its first premises **within two weeks** of opening (summer 2019), necessitating a full relocation to a larger venue — a fact cited across multiple media features.
